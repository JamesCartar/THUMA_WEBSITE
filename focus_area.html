Our Focus Area

Women's Healthcare

Healthcare is mostly neglected by underprivileged women since they can't afford both charges and time. Even the worst patients only take unauthorised medicine to get short term cure.

Gender-Based Violence Prevention and Response

Myanmar is recorded as one of the highest cased countries in Southeast Asia regarding with Gender-Based Violence. Thuma is trying to prevent and end the GBV for women in Myanmar.

Empowering Women Proficiency

According to the social norms of Myanmar, women have to take the role of looking after the household by neglecting the professional and educational development of herself. We, Thuma is working toward for women who want to chase their dreams proficiency.
